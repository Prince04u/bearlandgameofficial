Welcome to the prison

Bear Land Prison is our most addictive gamemode. The loop is simple — mine, sell, upgrade, repeat — but the depth comes from the systems layered on top: enchantments, prestige resets, PvP zones, rebirth tokens, and a sprawling endgame that keeps Prestige-90 players grinding alongside fresh A-rank inmates.

Start in Mine A with a wooden pickaxe and zero coins. By the time you reach Mine Z you'll be wielding a custom-enchanted Top Pickaxe with explosive perks, jackhammer charges, auto-sell modules and tokens to spare.

Mine progression

Each mine unlocks at a coin threshold. Better ores spawn at higher mines — Mine A is mostly stone and coal, Mine Z is end-game blocks worth thousands of coins each. Mines reset on a timer to prevent flat exhaustion, and the reset cadence gets faster as you climb.

Smart players don't just rush up the mines. They balance time spent at each tier to optimize coin-per-hour with their current pickaxe enchants, because over-leveling without gear can actually slow you down.

Prestige and rebirth

Hitting Mine Z is just the start. Prestiging resets your mine rank and gives you a permanent multiplier, a unique tag and access to prestige-only perks. Prestige 10 unlocks rebirth tokens, which let you reset prestige for even bigger meta-perks.

Long-term prestige rewards include rare cosmetics, exclusive cells, unique pet types and a leaderboard spot. Prestige 100 grants the legendary 'Free Bear' title — fewer than two dozen players have ever earned it.

PvP zones

Most of the prison is PvP-safe, but four optional arenas — the Yard, the Cafeteria, the Block-D Riot Zone and the Warden's Vault — drop premium loot for players willing to fight. Loot drops include enchant books, custom kits and rare token bundles.

Gear advantage matters but skill matters more. The PvP arenas have their own balance pass with capped enchant levels, so a fresh prestiger can absolutely take down a higher-prestige opponent who plays sloppy.

Daily life in prison

Daily reward chests, weekly missions, prestige milestones and a steady stream of seasonal events keep prison endlessly fresh. Our prison community is one of the most active on the network, with constant chatter, top-up advice for new players and casual races up the mines.

If you've ever loved a progression grind that rewards consistency, optimization and a little bit of PvP risk, this is the gamemode for you. Bring a pickaxe. Leave with a legacy.
