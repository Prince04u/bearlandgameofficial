Inside Events on Bear Land

Weekly events, seasonal competitions, community challenges and stacks of rewards.

Events are what make a network feel alive

On Bear Land, events are not an afterthought. We run a full event calendar with a dedicated events team, professionally streamed competitions, and prize pools that grow with the size of the participating field. Whether you have an hour to drop in or a weekend to compete, there is always something happening worth showing up for.

Our events span every gamemode. PvP gamemodes get tournaments and ladder seasons. Survival gets town-building contests and trade summits. Anarchy gets siege weekends. Even the lobby gets parkour races, hide-and-seek nights and trivia.

Weekly events

Every Friday at 20:00 UTC we kick off a flagship weekly event in rotation: Drop Party (lobby, free loot rain), KitPvP Cup (single-elimination brackets), Build-Off (themed 90-minute builds, community voting), and Boss Rush (co-op end-game bosses with stacked loot tables).

Saturdays add a regional event tuned for whichever timezone is busiest that week, and Sundays close out with our community-favourite Trivia & Treasure Hunt where players compete in cross-gamemode quizzes and parkour for BearCoin prizes.

Seasonal competitions

Each quarter we run a Season Pass-style competition with leaderboards in every major gamemode. Top finishers receive store credit, exclusive cosmetic sets that retire forever, and named entries on our permanent Hall of Champions. Mid-tier finishers still walk away with BearCoin payouts.

Seasonal competitions are designed so casual players can earn real rewards too. Every season has a participation track with milestones that everyone can hit, alongside the competitive track for top players. You don't have to be #1 to leave with something cool.

Community challenges

Community challenges are our favourite thing to run. We set a network-wide goal — for example, 'mine 50 million stone collectively this week' — and when the community hits it, everyone earns a free cosmetic, a BearCoin top-up or a network-wide XP boost.

These are the events that bring the most chat. Watching the counter tick up in lobby, seeing players hop between gamemodes to contribute, and unlocking together at the deadline is genuinely one of the highlights of being on the network.

How to participate

All events are announced in the in-game event board, the #events channel on Discord, and the news feed on this site. Just show up at the start time and use /event join in lobby, or follow the teleport command posted in chat.

Some events require sign-up brackets — those are posted at least 48 hours in advance on Discord. There is no entry fee for any event, and prizes are always paid out within 24 hours of the final.
