Inside Bedrock Edition on Bear Land

Cross-platform Minecraft. Mobile, Xbox, PlayStation, Switch and Windows all in one world.

Bear Land on Bedrock

We officially launched Bear Land Bedrock Edition in September 2025 after a year of internal testing. It is a full mirror of our Java network — Survival, Economy, KitPvP, SkyPvP and Events are all live — running on a dedicated Bedrock cluster with cross-region routing for low latency worldwide.

Joining is simple. Bedrock players use the address play.bear-land.net on port 19132. The first time you join, our hub assigns your account to a Bedrock-side profile that mirrors your Java progression for shared gamemodes, so cosmetics, ranks and BearCoin balances move with you.

Why play on Bedrock

Bedrock players get the smoothest possible Minecraft experience: native touch controls on mobile, controller layouts on console, and full Marketplace cosmetic integration. We've also customized our HUDs and chat UI to work cleanly on small screens without sacrificing competitive information.

Performance is the headline. Bedrock is built on the C++ engine, runs at higher framerates on the same hardware, and supports cross-region matchmaking. Our pings are tuned for under-80ms in EU, NA-East and NA-West, and under-150ms in OCE and SEA.

What gamemodes are available

Currently live on Bedrock: Survival, Economy, KitPvP, SkyPvP, and weekend Events. Anarchy and Prison are Java-only for technical reasons (specific plugin behavior that does not translate cleanly), but we are evaluating Bedrock-friendly equivalents for 2026.

All Bedrock gamemodes share our network-wide BearCoin economy and BearPrime subscription. Buying BearPrime once unlocks perks on both platforms, and your friends list, party invites and Discord linking all work cross-platform out of the box.

Mobile gameplay tips

Mobile players should enable Classic UI in Bedrock settings for a tighter HUD, raise their FOV to 80+, and enable split controls for better building. We strongly recommend using a Bluetooth controller for PvP gamemodes — touch works, but a controller closes the skill gap quickly.

Battery is the other consideration. Lower particle counts and render distance to 8 chunks on phones; you'll keep 60fps and double your play session length. Our hub menus are designed to be fully thumb-reachable so you don't need to stretch across the screen mid-match.

Joining your friends

Add friends using their Bear Land username (the same on both editions). Use /party invite in-game or send Discord invites via our linked-account system. Parties automatically queue together across gamemodes, and we'll never split a party across regions.

Cross-platform play means you can finally bring your console-locked friends into the network. Whether they're on Switch in the living room or PlayStation upstairs, they can join the same Survival town you've been building for months.
