Bear Land Network Rules

Read these carefully. By playing on Bear Land, you agree to follow them. Ignorance is not a defense.

Chat Rules

Be respectful to all players regardless of skill, language or background. We have zero tolerance for hate speech, slurs, harassment or doxxing.

No spam, no excessive caps, no advertising other networks. Keep promotion of personal channels and content reasonable.

English in global chat. Use language-specific Discord channels or party chat for other languages.

Staff decisions in chat are final. Argue them in DMs or appeals — never in public chat.

PvP Rules

In PvP-enabled zones, all combat is fair game unless explicitly stated otherwise.

No spawn camping in PvP gamemodes. Spawn protection lasts 10 seconds; deliberately killing during this window is not allowed.

Stream sniping during ranked or tournament matches is strictly forbidden.

Win-trading, ELO boosting and account sharing are all bannable offenses across all PvP gamemodes.

Exploits

Do not use any client-side modifications that grant gameplay advantages: x-ray, hacks, autoclickers, kill aura, reach mods, fly hacks, etc.

Do not abuse server bugs, dupe glitches or unintended item interactions. Report them via /bug or on the forum.

Macros for repetitive in-game tasks are allowed in non-PvP gamemodes within reason. Combat macros are banned everywhere.

Modifying clients for visual purposes only (Optifine, shaders, Lunar Client, Badlion Client) is fully allowed and encouraged.

Toxicity

No targeted harassment of individual players, including persistent griefing, stalking or vote-brigading.

No discussion of self-harm or content harmful to vulnerable players. Such discussion will result in immediate intervention.

Drama is a part of online communities. Keep it in private chats. Public escalation will be moderated.

Punishments

First-time minor offenses usually result in a warning or short mute. Repeated or severe offenses escalate to temporary or permanent bans.

Cheating (hacks, x-ray, etc.) carries an immediate 30-day ban on first offense and a permanent ban on second.

Major exploits, dupe abuse or any attempt to harm the server or other players results in an immediate permanent network-wide ban.

All punishments can be appealed through the forum. Appeals are reviewed within 72 hours by a different staff member from the one who issued the punishment.
