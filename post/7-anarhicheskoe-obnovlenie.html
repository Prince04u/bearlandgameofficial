Anarchy Update: Hardcore Mode

Brutal new hardcore mechanics, smarter PvP, and a deeper raiding meta for our most dedicated players.

Hardcore mechanics

Permadeath leaderboards are now live as an optional opt-in. Hardcore deaths log to a public memorial wall at spawn. Survive long enough on the hardcore ladder and unique cosmetics drop into your account.

PvP improvements

Crystal placement timing is more responsive. Totem pop reliability fixed. Combat logging now correctly tags loggers for two minutes regardless of method.

Raiding meta

TNT crafting rebalanced. New end-game raiding tools added. Base burial mechanics encourage deeper, more paranoid base design. Veterans should expect to relocate within the month.
