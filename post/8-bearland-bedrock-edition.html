Bear Land Bedrock Edition Launch

We're officially live on Bedrock. Mobile, Xbox, PlayStation, Switch and Windows 10 players can now join the full network.

How to join

Use play.bear-land.net on port 19132. Featured on the Marketplace server list under 'Featured Servers'.

Cross-platform support

Survival, Economy, KitPvP, SkyPvP and Events are all live on Bedrock. Your Bear Land account, cosmetics, BearCoin and ranks sync across Java and Bedrock automatically.

Mobile gameplay

UI is optimized for touch. Controller support is full. We've tuned regional routing for under-80ms latency in EU and NA peak. Mobile players, welcome home.
