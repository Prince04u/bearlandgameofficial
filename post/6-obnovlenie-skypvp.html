Major SkyPvP Update

New rewards, sharper performance, rebalanced PvP mechanics — the biggest SkyPvP patch of the year.

Performance

Tick rate is now held above 19.8 in 99% of matches. Map streaming was rewritten to cut join time by 60%. Hit registration latency is down to sub-90ms in EU and NA.

New rewards

Season 8 rewards include a brand new Bear Master crown cosmetic, three new seasonal capes for top divisions, and Prime-only particle trails.

Balance

Diamond armor durability rebalanced. Pearl cooldown standardized. Two underperforming kits buffed (Bowman, Crystal Mage), one overperforming kit nerfed (Berserker). Full patch notes on the forum.
