Introducing BearPrime

Our new premium membership: monthly rewards, exclusive cosmetics, priority access and a stack of perks.

Premium membership

BearPrime is $9.99/month, cancellable anytime. Designed around our no-pay-to-win promise — perks are cosmetic, convenience, and earning speed only.

Monthly rewards

Guaranteed BearCoin top-up, a Prime-exclusive cosmetic, crate keys and access to monthly Prime-only events. Rewards roll in automatically on the 1st of every month.

Exclusive cosmetics

200+ Prime cosmetics already live, with a new one added every month. Some retire after their month — long-term Prime members build a unique veteran collection nobody else can match.
