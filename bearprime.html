BearPrime — Premium Membership

Monthly perks, exclusive cosmetics, priority access and special rewards for premium members.

Premium membership, the right way

BearPrime is our monthly premium subscription. It's designed around one rule: never sell competitive advantage. Everything BearPrime includes is either cosmetic, convenience, or earning speed — never in-game power.

If you love Bear Land, want to support the network, and want a steady stream of cosmetics, BearCoin top-ups and priority perks for your monthly support, BearPrime is built for you.

Monthly rewards

Every month, BearPrime members get a guaranteed BearCoin top-up, a Prime-exclusive cosmetic, a stack of crate keys, and access to that month's Prime-only event. Reward value is designed to exceed the subscription cost for active players.

Rewards roll in automatically on the first of each month. There's no claim window, no expiration, no hoops to jump through. Open the game, see the notification, pick up your reward.

Exclusive cosmetics

BearPrime members get access to the entire Prime cosmetic catalogue — over 200 items as of this update, with a new cosmetic added every month. These include particle trails, victory animations, pet skins, weapon visuals and lobby gadgets.

Some Prime cosmetics retire after their month. Once retired, they never return to the store. Long-term Prime members accumulate a unique cosmetic collection that visibly marks them as veterans of the network.

Priority access

BearPrime gives you priority queue access during peak hours. You'll never wait in a full-server queue again. We reserve a portion of every server's slots specifically for Prime members so you can always log in.

You also get priority access to event signups, beta tests, and early-access features. New gamemodes and major updates often go to Prime members first for a week before opening to everyone.

Special perks

BearPrime members get a unique chat tag, exclusive Discord roles, double daily rewards, +20% BearCoin earning across every gamemode, +100% job XP, extra home points in Survival, and Prime-only PvP cosmetic loadouts.

You also get private access to the Prime Discord channels where staff and developers share previews, gather feedback, and run small Prime-only events and giveaways throughout the month.

Pricing and flexibility

BearPrime is $9.99 per month, with discounts for quarterly and annual subscriptions. You can cancel anytime from your account dashboard — there are no contracts and no cancellation fees. If you cancel, your perks continue until the end of your paid period.

Gift subscriptions are available. You can gift BearPrime to a friend for a month, three months, or a year, and they'll get every perk just like a paying member. Gifts are a popular way to bring friends onto the network.

Why subscribe

The honest answer: subscriptions help us fund the network. Server hardware, developer salaries, community events and event prize pools are all paid for by store revenue. BearPrime is the single biggest support method players have.

In return, you get more cosmetics, more BearCoin, more events, more access. If you play Bear Land regularly and want to see it keep growing, BearPrime is the most direct way to make that happen — and you get a stack of cool stuff out of it.
