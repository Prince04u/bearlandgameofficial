Privacy Policy

How Bear Land collects, uses and protects your data. Last updated: November 2025.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Bear Land ('we', 'us', 'our') collects, uses and protects information when you use our Minecraft network and website.

Information we collect

Account data: Minecraft username, UUID, IP address, login timestamps, and any information you submit to our forum or Discord.

Gameplay data: in-game actions, chat messages (for moderation), inventory snapshots, statistics and rankings.

Purchase data: when you buy from our store, our payment processor collects and processes payment details. We never see or store your card details.

How we use information

To operate the network, deliver gameplay features, process purchases, enforce our rules, and improve our services.

To send important account or service announcements. We do not send marketing emails without explicit opt-in.

To comply with legal obligations and respond to law enforcement requests where required by law.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers necessary to run the network (hosting, payment processing, analytics) and only the minimum necessary.

We may publish aggregated, anonymized statistics (e.g. total players online, top gamemodes) for community updates.

Data retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@bear-land.net.

Chat logs are retained for 90 days for moderation and dispute resolution.

Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by accounting and tax law.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export and delete your personal data. Submit requests via privacy@bear-land.net.

EU users have additional rights under GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children

Bear Land is intended for users 13 and older. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal data, contact privacy@bear-land.net and we will delete it.

Updates

This policy may be updated. Material changes will be announced in-game and on this site at least 14 days before they take effect.

Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@bear-land.net
