FAQ

Common questions

What versions of Minecraft do you support?

We support Java 1.16 through the latest release, plus full Bedrock Edition cross-play on mobile, console and Windows 10.

How do I join the server?

Add play.bear-land.net to your multiplayer servers list. From the Bedrock app, use port 19132.

Is Bear Land free to play?

Yes! Every gamemode is 100% free. The store is optional and supports development.

What is BearCoin?

Our in-network currency. Earn it daily, win it from events, or buy packages to unlock cosmetics, kits and perks.

How do I report a player?

Open a report on the forum or contact a moderator directly on our Discord.
