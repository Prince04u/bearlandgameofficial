BearCoin — The Currency of Bear Land

Everything you need to know about BearCoin, our universal network currency.

What is BearCoin?

BearCoin is the official in-network currency of Bear Land. It powers our store, fuels our events, and drives the player-to-player economy across every gamemode. Whether you're cashing out a Prison prestige reward, buying a new cosmetic, or tipping a friend who helped you build a town — it all runs on BearCoin.

BearCoin is account-bound, transferable between gamemodes, and never expires. Your balance follows you across every Bear Land world, from Bedrock to Java, from KitPvP to Survival, with a single shared wallet.

How to earn BearCoin

You can earn BearCoin through nearly every action on the network. Mining, farming and completing jobs in Survival and Economy generate steady income. Winning matches in KitPvP and SkyPvP pays out per-kill and per-victory bonuses. Reaching mine ranks in Prison rewards you with bulk BearCoin drops. Even daily login streaks contribute.

Beyond gameplay, you can earn BearCoin through community contribution — building feature submissions, hosting community events, helping new players in the welcome lobby, and writing news posts for our community blog. We believe contribution should be rewarded.

Daily rewards

Every player gets a daily login chest with BearCoin and small bonus rolls. Streaks pay out compounding rewards — log in seven days straight for a major bonus, fourteen for a rare cosmetic chance, thirty for a guaranteed legendary cosmetic and a major BearCoin payout.

BearPrime members get an enhanced daily reward with double base BearCoin, additional cosmetic rolls and access to a separate Prime-only chest with exclusive rewards. The streak resets independently per gamemode so you're never punished for focusing on one mode.

Events rewards

Events are the biggest BearCoin payout opportunities on the network. Weekly events pay out thousands of BearCoin to top finishers. Seasonal competitions pay tens of thousands. Major tournaments push into the hundreds of thousands for first place finishers.

Even just participating earns BearCoin. Every event has a participation reward so casual players who show up still walk away with something. We design event payouts to reward both top performance and consistent attendance.

Store usage

BearCoin is the primary currency in the in-game store. Use it to buy cosmetics, pets, kits, crate keys, and exclusive seasonal items. Prices are set in BearCoin and rebalanced quarterly based on player feedback and economic data.

Some store items can only be bought with BearCoin earned in-game — including all competitive cosmetics. We do this to keep prestige rewards meaningful for players who earn them through skill rather than purchase.

Premium benefits

BearPrime members get a permanent 20% BearCoin earning bonus across every gamemode, plus monthly BearCoin top-ups. Combined with double daily rewards, BearPrime accounts typically accumulate three to four times the BearCoin of free accounts.

If you're serious about climbing leaderboards, building a Survival empire, or topping the store cosmetic charts, BearPrime is the smartest way to accelerate your progression without compromising fairness — BearPrime never grants competitive advantages, only earning speed.

Buying BearCoin

You can buy BearCoin in packages from our store, with bonus BearCoin scaling on larger packages. Bulk purchases include free cosmetic crate keys and limited-edition tags. All BearCoin purchases are credited instantly to your account.

We never sell competitive advantages. You cannot buy gear, kits, or in-game power. BearCoin only unlocks cosmetics, convenience perks, and items you could otherwise earn through play. We're proud of this — and we're never going to change it.
